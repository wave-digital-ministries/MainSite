Surf Writers - Conduct

Our Code of Conduct

Failure to abide by these policies may result in a ban or suspension from club attendance.

We do not permit an "Not Safe For Work" (NSFW) to be presented or discussed within our clubs. (Philippians 4:8)

Use common sense and do not promote offensive behavior or language. 

Be respectful of others and avoid offensive behavior or language.

We obligate our chapter leaders and members to abide by Copy Right laws and to not plagiarize. (Exodus 20:2-17)

Welcome non-Christians to club meetings and events. This is an outreach, not a clique.

We do not permit offensive or explicit imagery from being present within our clubs.
