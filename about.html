About Us

Our Mission

Wave Discipleship Ministries' mission is to share His Word, tend to His Creation, and #BeAWave4Christ through technology and community.

Our Story

Founded as Wave Digital Ministries in 2019, Wave Discipleship Ministries has reached over 150,000 people across 33 nations.

Originally focused as a web-based ministry, WDM produced educational content for web and social media. However, once entering the post-COVID era it became clear that God was directing the ministry to reshape and refocus towards in-person ministry.

We have only just begun the process of refocusing and building an in-person ministry, but are hopeful for what God has in store for this new season!

Our Values

Faith

We believe that we are saved through faith in Jesus Christ.

Our ministry is non-denominational. While we are not a member of any denomination we will work with denominational organizations.

Academia

We believe that one of the waves we can make for Christ is through academia.

We are working to produce Biblical education resources as well as building a campus outreach program.

Community

We are called to be a church that meets together.

During COVID, we became accustomed in not meeting together as a church. We must return to meeting together as a church in person.

Stewardship

Per Genesis 1:26, we believe that it is our duty as Christians to tend to the Lord's Creation and be good stewards of what He has entrusted us with.
